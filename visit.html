Plan Your Visit

Location

The museum is located at

Øynavegen 60

Inderøy, Norway

Admission

The entrance is free for all.

Guided tours are available at 70 NOK per person, including a handy printed guide of the museum.

Contact us to arrange a guided tour for groups of 6 or more people.

Saturday 9:00 AM - 4:00 PM
Sunday 9:00 AM - 1:00 PM

Accessibility

Wheelchair Accessability

The museum has wheelchair accessibility ramps

Visually Impaired

Audio guides and braille display signs are available for the visually impaired.

Food and Drink

There is a café attached to the museum where you can get light lunches, soft drinks, coffee, snacks and more.

Shop

Our shop offers a range of memorabilia from the museum as well as great gifts and activity packs that allow you to continue to explore science even after you’ve left the museum.
